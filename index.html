About Me

I am a Data Scientist and Professor of Statistics with over 24 years of experience in teaching, research, and public policy. My work focuses on statistical modeling, data science, and the development of R packages to address complex real-world problems.

I have proudly served as the Chairman of the Department of Mathematics & Statistics at the University of Agriculture, Faisalabad, where I initiated the PhD (Statistics), BS (Statistics), and BS (Mathematics) programs.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences, Clemson University, USA. I have also worked as Team Lead of the Policy & Research Unit (PRU) at BISP, Pakistan, under the Asian Development Bank (ADB), where I designed and evaluated evidence-based social protection strategies.
